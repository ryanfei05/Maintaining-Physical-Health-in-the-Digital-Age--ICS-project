About the Site & Author:

The purpose of this online website is to educate others about the impact that digital technology has on our physical health. It provides ways to prevent possible issues from arising, and info on maintaining physical health in the present digital day.
The author, Ryan Fei is a student of Marc Garneau Collegiate Institute, learning web development at ICS4U1.
